A Model of the Cubic Earth

When visualizing the earth as a cube, it can be difficult to avoid simply translating a model of the globe into the shape of a cube. This approach is absolutely ridiculous and is almost as unscientific as depicting the earth as a sphere in the first place. In the current Cubic Earth model, all of our known continents are located on the top face of the earth, with antarctica bordering the edges, preventing the oceans from flowing over the edges of the cube. Proof of Flat Ground Surface

Using elementary measurements, it is clearly seen that the surface of the earth is naturally flat. As seen on the level device, the bubble is perfectly in the center. If the ground was curved as with a sphere, the level would not measure a flat surface.
